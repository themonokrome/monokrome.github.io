Mitochondria - The Power House of Okhla Industrial Area.

Mitochondria the power house of cell which is vital for proper functioning of the cell and thus the human body able to do the needful. In the same way these industrial slums are the power house for the factories which on broader level helps to develop the nation, but no concrete action take place to ensure their safety and conditions.

Written by Zeeshan Ibrar, New Delhi, February 26, 2016
